Demonstrate Outcomes

Project Motivation:

Our group has a good understanding of HTML, we are interested in gaming product and we want to create a online game store to have discount to the client, the client can get different discounts by ranking the game, our motivation is to give customers who love to play games to win more discounts by playing our web games, so that customers will be more enthusiastic about the game and feel more interesting.

Features:

[Description of Feature]

The login & registration: As a new user, I want to register a new account, then I go to the register pages to fill out my personal information in a list fields of registration form. After registering I can enter my username and password to login into this website to buy the games.

[Description of validation test]

We use this feature to identify the client’s information, when the client register and login we will know who the user is, when they play games, they will be the user name appears and if they win a discount, we will be able to get his information easily.

[Description of Feature]

Online game: As a player who likes to play games, I want to try the interesting web game in order to win a discount, so I can go to the game's interface to participate and have lots of fun.

[Description of validation test]

When the user clicks on play the game, they have chances to win different discounts when they check out. So that the clients don’t just buy games, they can get a discount when they enjoy playing games.

[Description of Feature]

Blog: As a user who likes to participate in discussions, I want to go to the Blog page, so that I can have a conversation with other users to share my ideas. I also want to write some issues or problems on this page, so that the other players can help me to figure it out.

[Description of validation test]

BThe user can give us some feedback about our website, or they can have a chat in the blog about introduction to the game, share different opinions, we will check the blog each week to see their feedback.

[Description of Feature]

Purchase products: As a customer, I want to buy some game products, after I place the order. So I go to the purchase page to fill out my order information and check out my game product.

[Description of validation test]

The user can check out the game product through the purchase page and pay for the product that they want. Then enter the details before delivery, such as full name, address, email, phone number etc

[Description of Feature]

Ranking list: As a customer, I want to win a discount to purchase my products, so after I play the games I can go to rank list to see an ordered list published on the basis of statistical results, so that I can win a discount for my product.

[Description of validation test]

We are setting a ranking list in order to know which player plays the best, and then we will give them a corresponding discount, we will update our ranking list every day, in this way more players will actively participate in our games.

Extended Viable Features

[Description of Feature]

Contact table: As a new visitor, I want to talk to individuals with this web organization and ask some questions, so I can go to the Contact us page to see they provide for me with information about how to contact them.

[Description of validation test]

We will provide our information on the contact us page so that the clients can ask some questions or problems through this page. They will need to fill out the information and message on the contact table. We will check it everyday in order to solve their issues.

[Description of Feature]

Search: As a customer, I want to look for a game product or something similar but I don’t know where it is. so I can search relevant information on Search, then the related product appears for me.

[Description of validation test]

We use this feature where customers can search keywords to find the product they want. It's a more convenient and faster way through search. Search aims to improve the speed of people to obtain and collect information, and provides a better network environment for people.

[Description of Feature]

Navigation: As a new client, I want to go to different pages to visit this online store. So I can use a navigation bar to easily access the content that I need, I can quickly transfer from one page to another when browsing a website.

[Description of validation test]

The navigation bars appear on each page of the website, the client can go to different pages to find what they are looking for through navigation. It is a faster and easy way for users to use.
